Hello !

I'm Antara. I also go by the name Kou on social media!

I'm a Comp Science Undergrad student, trying to make an impact with illustrations and design in between my monotonous life of trying to fix my code.

I have experience as an illustrator and graphic designer, by learning design as a highschool senior and eventually, working amongst various people at University. My illustrations are heavily inspired by Japanese mangas and animes that have influenced me since a young age. I've worked on diverse projects, including logos, event posters & banners, event brochures, package design and social media posts.

I believe that design has the power to influence and change people's opinion on various things, even the smallest day-to-day object one buys for their house.

Feel free to explore my portfolio and reach out for collaborations or inquiries. Thank you for visiting!

P.S. You can probably tell that I love cats by the theme of this portfolio...This is also my first time building a proper website from scratch so please just enjoy the cats haha ♡
